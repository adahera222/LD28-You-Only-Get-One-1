About YOGO

YOGO was made with a bit of care and a lot of apathy for Ludum Dare 28 by mathphreak and using:

  • Twitter Bootstrap
  • Font Awesome
  • jQuery
  • underscore.js
  • Backbone.js
  • Sublime Text 3
  • Grunt

This is my first Ludum Dare entry, so give me a break, OK?

This game is dedicated to the victims of the Arapahoe High School shooting on December 13, 2013.

YOGO

You have three attributes: health, strength, and sanity. All three are automatically tracked by the game, but you only get one of them that you can see.

Choose your stat!

Health

Your health is the most vital attribute; when you reach 0 health, you die.

Strength

Strength controls how quickly you kill enemies, which in turn controls how much damage you take while in combat.

Sanity

Only having two attributes felt lame, so I had to add a third one; this doesn't really impact much, nor does it change drastically throughout the game, but getting to 0 is bad.
